Watermelon Feta Salad

Description

This is a chunky watermelon feta salad with arugula and tomatoes.

Thanks to the watermelon, everyone like it, kids too!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ouunces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar and salt together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. add tomatoes, arugula and red onion; toss to coat
  3. gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese to serve
